Fees and discounts

- Tuition payment is due the 1st of each month. Late fee of $10 will be added if the payment is not received by 7th of the month. Tuition fees are not refundable. Any missed classes due to illness, vacation, or for any other reason are not recommended or refunded, however, make-up classes are offered regularly to all students (please ask the coach for the next open make-up class). Exceptions might be made in certain cases with proper advance notice.
- Sibling discount is offered at 15% for the membership.
